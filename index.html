Mark Zuckerberg

Mark Elliot Zuckerberg is an American media magnate, internet entrepreneur, and philanthropist.

The life line of Mark Elliot Zuckerberg:

  • 1984 - Mark Elliot Zuckerberg was born May 14, 1984 in White Plains, New York.
  • 2004 -He his an American media magnate, internet entrepreneur, and philanthropist. He is known for co-founding Facebook, Inc. and serves as its chairman, chief executive officer, and controlling shareholder.
  • 2004 -Zuckerberg attended Harvard University, And launched the Facebook social networking service from his dormitory room on February 4, 2004, with college roommates Eduardo Saverin, Andrew McCollum, Dustin Moskovitz, and Chris Hughes.
  • 2012 -Facebook Was Originally launched to select college campuses, the site expanded rapidly and eventually beyond colleges, reaching one billion users by 2012.
  • 2012 - Zuckerberg took the company public in May 2012 with majority shares.
  • 2007 - In 2007, at age 23, he became the world's youngest self-made billionaire. As of October 2021, Zuckerberg's net worth is $122 billion, making him the 5th-richest person in the world.
